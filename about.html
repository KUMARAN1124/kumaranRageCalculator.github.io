About

An Age Calculator is a tools used for calulating age of a person based his birthdate relative to current date. An Age calculator is fast and efficient to calculate someone's age.

This type of Tool can be used in many places like:

  1. HealthCare
  2. Education
  3. Theaters/Malls
  4. Legal Matters
  5. Online Services

Instructions

Step1: Select Your Birth Date


Step2: Verify whether you have              entered it correctly


Step 3: Click the "Calculate               Age" button


Step 4: If You want to reset the               selected age click on               the "Reset Date" button


Step 5: See Your Results!!
